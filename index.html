What is Coachella?

Coachella was founded in 1999 by Paul Tollett and Goldenvoice after being inspired by a 1993 Pearl Jam concert at the Empire Polo Club in Indio, California. The event aimed to bring together diverse music genres and large-scale art installations in a desert setting. Originally a response to the rise of alternative festivals and dissatisfaction with the commercial nature of mainstream music events, Coachella has since evolved into one of the worldâ€™s most iconic music and arts festivals, attracting major artists and fans from around the globe.
